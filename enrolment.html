What to Expect

A Warm & Gentle Transition

We recognise that every child settles in their own time. Our educators work closely with you to tailor the transition so your child feels safe, confident, and happy.

  • Two Complimentary Transition Visits

    Each visit lasts up to one hour, scheduled around your child's start date, allowing them to experience the daily flow and begin forming relationships.

  • Flexible & Supportive

    Visits are flexible and designed around your family. Our goal is for your child to feel comfortable and at ease before their official start.

  • Open Communication

    Daily updates, learning stories, and regular parent-teacher conversations keep you connected to your child's journey.

  • Individual Learning Plans

    Every child receives a personalised learning plan tailored to their interests, strengths, and developmental needs.
