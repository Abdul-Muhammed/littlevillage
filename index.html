Our Philosophy

Learning Through Play, Relationships & Nature

We believe children thrive when they feel safe, respected, and nurtured. Our approach values play, exploration, and relationships as the foundation for learning.

Te Whāriki & Montessori Inspired

Guided by Te Whāriki and child-centred approaches including Montessori principles and Vygotsky's social learning theory.

Whole-Child Development

Supporting emotional, social, physical, and cognitive wellbeing through a warm, inclusive, and responsive environment.

Transition-to-School Programme

A purposeful programme that supports children to become confident, independent, and self-regulated learners for school.

A Day in Our Village

A Gentle Rhythm of Care & Learning

Every day at Little Village is thoughtfully structured to balance active play, guided learning, rest, and nourishment.

  • Early Morning

    Warm Welcome & Breakfast

    Supporting smooth transitions, sharing kai in a calm, familiar setting.

  • Mid Morning

    Stories, Songs & Exploration

    Group time, messy play, sensory exploration, and indoor-outdoor flow.

  • Midday

    Rolling Lunch & Calm Yoga

    Shared routines that promote self-help skills and self-regulation.

  • Afternoon

    Learning & Tidy-Up

    Spontaneous learning, collaborative routines building responsibility.
